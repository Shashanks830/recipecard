Biryani

Aromatic layered rice dish with tender meat and rich spices.
🥣 Serves 4 ⏱️ 75 Mins 🔥 Medium Spice

Ingredients

The Base
  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 500g Chicken/Mutton, cut into pieces
  • 1/2 cup Yogurt
  • 1 lemon, 3/4 juiced and remaining 1/4 in 2 slices
  • 2 cups of oil
  • 1/4 cup Ghee (can use butter)
Fresh Produce
  • 2/3 large onions, thinly sliced (around 2 to 3 mm)
  • 4-5 Green chilies crushed or finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp Fresh coriander, chopped
  • 2 tbsp Fresh mint, chopped
Spice Box
  • 2 tbsp Ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp Red chili powder (non-spicy)
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp Cumin powder
  • 1 tsp Coriander powder
  • 1 tsp Garam masala powder or Biryani masala
  • Salt to taste
Whole Spices
  • 1 tsp Cumin seeds
  • 2-3 Bay leaves
  • 3-4 Green cardamom pods
  • 1 Black cardamom pod
  • 1 inch Cinnamon stick
  • 4-5 Cloves
  • 1 star anise

Cooking Process

01. Prep Rice
Soak basmati rice in water for 30 minutes. Boil until 70% cooked, drain and set aside.
02. Fry Onions
Heat ghee in a heavy-bottomed pot. Fry onions until golden brown. Remove half and set aside for garnish.
03. Cook Meat
In the remaining onions, add ginger-garlic paste, green chilies, and cook for 1 minute. Add yogurt and meat. Cook on high heat for 2 minutes, then medium heat for 10 minutes.
04. Layer Rice
Layer the partially cooked rice over the meat mixture. Sprinkle fried onions, mint, and coriander on top.
05. Add Spices
Place whole spices (cumin seeds, bay leaves, cardamom, cinnamon, cloves, star anise) on the rice.
Dum Method: Cover with aluminum foil, then place the lid. Cook on high heat for 2 minutes until steam forms. Reduce heat to low and cook for 30-35 minutes.
06. Rest & Serve
Turn off heat and let it rest for 5 minutes without opening the lid. Open carefully and mix gently with a fork. Serve hot.
Made with ❤️ · Best served hot with raita or salad.
