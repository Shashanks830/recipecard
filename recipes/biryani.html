Biryani

Aromatic layered rice dish with tender meat and rich spices.
🥣 Serves 4 ⏱️ 75 Mins 🔥 Medium Spice

Ingredients

The Base
  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 500g Chicken/Mutton, cut into pieces
  • 1/2 cup Yogurt
  • 1 lemon, 3/4 juiced and remaining 1/4 in 2 slices
  • 2 cups of oil
  • 1/4 cup Ghee (can use butter)
Fresh Produce
  • 2/3 large onions, thinly sliced (around 2 to 3 mm)
  • 4-5 Green chilies crushed or finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp Fresh coriander, chopped (if available)
  • 2 tbsp Fresh mint, chopped(if available)
Spice Box
  • 2 tbsp Ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp Red chili powder (non-spicy)
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp Cumin powder
  • 1 tsp Coriander powder
  • 1 tsp Garam masala powder or Biryani masala
  • Salt to taste
Whole Spices
  • 1 tsp Cumin seeds
  • 2-3 Bay leaves
  • 3-4 Green cardamom pods
  • 1 Black cardamom pod
  • 1 inch Cinnamon stick
  • 4-5 Cloves
  • 1 star anise (optional)

Cooking Process

01. Prep Onion Birista
Heat oil(or ghee) in a pan. Fry sliced onions until golden brown. They should be crispy and caramelized. Donot overcook. Remove half and set aside for garnish.
02. Marination
Take chicken/mutton in a bowl. Add spices like turmeric powder, coriander powder, red chili powder, cumin powder, garam masala powder, salt, ginger-garlic paste, green chilies, onion birista, freshly chopped mint and coriander leaves and yogurt. Mix well and let it marinate for at least 30 minutes. (Ideally, marinate for 2-4 hours or overnight in the refrigerator for best results).
03. Prep Rice
Soak basmati rice in water for 30 minutes. Put whole spices and sliced lemon in the water with some oil(or ghee) from birista and bring to roaring boil. Add the soaked rice and let it boil until 60% cooked, drain and set aside.
04. Cook Meat
In a wide bottom vessel, heat some oil or ghee. Add the marinated meat and cook on high heat for 5-7 minutes until the meat is browned. Reduce heat to medium, cover and cook for 5-10 minutes until the meat is tender and the oil starts to separate from the gravy. Adjust salt and spices if needed. remove some of the rogan (oil) from the top and set aside for layering.
04. Layer Rice
Layer the partially cooked rice over the cooked meat mixture. Sprinkle remaining fried onions, rogan, mint, and coriander on top. If you want you can also do multilayer.
Dum Method: Cover with aluminum foil, then place the lid. Cook on high heat for 2 minutes until steam forms. Reduce heat to low and cook for 10 minutes.
06. Rest & Serve
Turn off heat and let it rest for 5 minutes without opening the lid. Open carefully and mix gently with a fork. Serve hot.
Made with ❤️ · Best served hot with raita or salad.
